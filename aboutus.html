How We Started

Jason's Chassis Shop started out as a small side gig in Jason's home garage. After he finished welding school, he started JCS as a grassroots fab shop with the intent to just do some light fab work. That all changed when a friend asked him to help him with a weld-in 8.50 cage for his Mustang. After finishing that cage he knew that was what he wanted to do. JCS is now the home of 15 fabricators and is based in a 100,000 square foot shop in North Carolina. We do more than just chassis work, you can order your own custom rear end with ladder bar or 4 link brackets here!

Jason started out with nothing but a passion and this little Harbor Freight welder. It's a testament to where hard work can get you!
